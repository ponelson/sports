Methodology

Values derived from public reporting through May 2026. Sources include Sportico, Forbes, CNBC team valuations, league announcements, SEC filings, press releases, and trade publications. Purchase prices for control stakes; minority deals noted separately where applicable.

Coverage

Five views: Media rights (31 leagues), team owners (123 teams), venues (31 facilities), naming rights (81 deals), and a chat interface for natural-language queries. Scope spans NFL, NBA, WNBA, NHL, MLB, MLS, EPL, La Liga, F1, MotoGP, SailGP, NASCAR, UFC/WWE.

Limitations

Many minority owners (especially in NFL/NBA limited partnerships) are not publicly disclosed. Stadium "owner" reflects legal title; operating control may rest with team holding companies under long-term leases. Currency conversions reflect approximate USD as of May 2026.
